Grandma's Secret Apple pie

this apple pie recipie has been in my family for generations, and never fail to delight.

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup suger
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium size apples, peeled and sliced

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375 degree farenheight
  2. in a bowl mix the cinnamon and sugar
  3. toss the apples in the cinnamon-sugar mixture
